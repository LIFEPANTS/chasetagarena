Game Features

🎮

8 Players Local

Connect up to 8 players! No need for extra controllers—everyone uses their own device as both a controller and a viewport for their personal perspective.

🌐

Totally Offline

Play anywhere, anytime. Chase Tag Arena requires zero internet connection. Just hop on the same local Wi-Fi or hotspot and start the chase.

📺

TV Spectator Mode

Connect a TV or large screen to the host device for an epic cinematic view. Let spectators cheer on the action while you dominate the arena!

🏗️

Level Editor

Build your own arenas with the powerful built-in editor. Place obstacles, traps, and power-ups anywhere—even while offline.

📱

Cross-Platform

Play seamlessly on Phone, Tablet, PC, or Mac. Every device works together in the same arena.

Get Started Guide

1

Install the App

Download Chase Tag Arena from the Google Play Store on your favorite devices.

2

Join the Lobby

Make sure everyone is on the same Wi-Fi network. One player hosts a game, and others can join instantly.

3

Choose Your Arena

Select from our default professional maps or dive into the community's custom creations.

4

Run, Jump, Tag!

Use simple controls to outmaneuver your friends. First one to tag everyone wins!
