Chase Tag Arena - Official User Guide

Welcome to Chase Tag Arena! The ultimate, fast-paced local multiplayer game where you outmaneuver your friends in intense games of tag. Because our game has some unique mechanics compared to traditional games, we've created this guide to get you up and running quickly.

1. What is Chase Tag Arena?

Chase Tag Arena is a Local Area Network (LAN) Multiplayer game. This means you don't connect to remote servers over the internet. Instead, you play directly with the friends who are in the same room (or on the same Wi-Fi network) as you. It's an "offline multiplayer" experience designed for zero-latency, high-energy fun.

Important: All players must be connected to the exact same Wi-Fi network or mobile hotspot to play together.

2. Quick Start: Your First Match

Getting started is easy. As long as one person has downloaded the app, everyone else can join in!

For the Host (The person setting up the game):

  1. Open the Chase Tag Arena App.
  2. Tap the "Host Game" button.
  3. Your device is now acting as the game server! You will see a Lobby screen with a QR code and a connection URL (e.g., http://192.168.1.5:3000).

For the Players (Joining the game):

  1. Ensure you are connected to the same Wi-Fi network as the Host.
  2. If you have the app installed, open it and tap "Join Game". It should automatically find the host!
  3. Don't have the app? No problem! Open the camera on your iOS or Android device and scan the QR code on the Host's screen. This will open the game directly in your web browser. (Note: You can also play on a PC/Mac by typing the connection URL into your browser).
Pro Tip: While playing through the web browser works great, downloading the native app from the App Store or Google Play provides a significantly smoother, faster, and more robust experience compared to the browser-based Web App (PWA).

Adding Bots

Not enough players? Or just want more chaos? The Host can add AI bots to the game by pressing the "Add Bot" button in the lobby before starting the match.

3. Game Modes

Chase Tag Arena features a couple of exciting game modes:

  • Classic Tag: One player starts as "It". Their goal is to tag another player to pass it on. The player who is "It" the least amount of time wins.
  • Infection (Zombie Tag): One player starts infected. Every player they tag also becomes infected. The last uninfected survivor wins!

4. Rules, Powerups, and Objectives

  • Movement: Use your device screen as a controller (joystick on the left, action buttons on the right) to run, slide, and jump across the arena.
  • Tagging: To tag someone, simply run into them while you are "It".
  • Powerups: Keep an eye out for glowing items on the map! Collecting these can grant you temporary speed boosts, jump height increases, or the ability to freeze opponents. Use them strategically.
  • Objectives: Depending on the game mode, either avoid the "It" player at all costs or, if you are "It", hunt down the nearest player using the environment to cut them off.

5. The Arena Editor

Unleash your creativity with the built-in Arena Editor! You can access this from the main menu.

  • Build: Place walls, obstacles, bounce pads, and spawn points anywhere on the grid.
  • Save & Play: Save your custom arenas offline and host them for your friends to play on.
  • Settings: Inside the editor, you can adjust map bounds, lighting, and environmental effects to set the perfect mood for your chase.

6. General Settings

From the main menu, tap the Settings icon to access customizable options:

  • Joystick Sensitivity: Adjust how fast your character turns and moves.
  • Graphics Quality: Lower this setting to improve frame rates on older devices.
  • Sound & Music: Toggle background beats and sound effects.
