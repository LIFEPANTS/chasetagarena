Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Chase Tag Arena. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our game and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. Data We Collect

Chase Tag Arena is designed to be a local multiplayer game. We do not collect any personal identification information (PII) from you. We do not require account creation or registration.

We may collect non-personal data such as device information (model, OS version) and gameplay statistics to help us improve the game experience.

3. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services like Google Play Services for achievements, leaderboards, or cloud saves. These services may collect information used to identify you. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is intended for a general audience. Since the game does not have any functionality to input, transmit, or store personal data, it is impossible for any user, including children, to provide us with personal information. We do not knowingly collect, nor are we technically able to receive, any personal data from children.

5. Offline Play

Chase Tag Arena is designed to work entirely offline. An internet connection is not required to play the game, host a local lobby, or use the level editor. Any data used for local multiplayer remains on your local network.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us via our developer page or community channels.
